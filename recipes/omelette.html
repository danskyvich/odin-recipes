Omelette

Description

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Beat three eggs in a bowl and mixed until well stirred.

  2. In a pan, sautee tomatoes in hot oil until soft. After that, pour in the beaten eggs.

  3. Season using pepper, salt and paprika. Wait for 5 minutes.

  4. Flip the egg to its other side and wait for 3 minutes. Take it out of the pan and serve it hot. Garnish if necessary.
