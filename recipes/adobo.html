Adobo

Description

Ingredients











Steps

  1. Prepare the marinade. If the chicken is too large, cut it into smaller pieces. Soak the meat into a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, calamansi that has been squished, red peppers, and whole peppercorns. Soak it for atmost 1 hour.

  2. After one hour, prepare the stove and the pan. Fry the garlic until brown and then fry the meat. Leave the marinade to the side.

  3. If the chicken is brown, pour the marinade in. Add the laurel leaves and Knorr cubes.

  4. Leave the pan to boil.
